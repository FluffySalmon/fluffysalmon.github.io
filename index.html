About

Melodies made for midnight headphones and glowing city stages.

Chloe Chan is a fictional Singapore-based musician blending indie pop, dream soul, and cinematic electronic textures. Her music explores memory, longing, self-discovery, and the strange magic of late-night conversations.

Inspired by artists like Laufey, The Japanese House, FKA twigs, and wave-lit synthpop records, Chloe creates elegant songs with intimate vocals, lush harmonies, and atmospheric production.
