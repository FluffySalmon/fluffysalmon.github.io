About the Artist

Where film score meets live performance.

Artist Photo

Chloe Chan is currently a recent graduate under Berklee College of Music, recently receiving a Bachelor Degree of Arts in Music Composition for Film, TV and Games. She started playing piano at 4 years old, and passed various exams in ABRSM and YAMAHA since then.

During secondary school, Chloe joined the Symphonic Band CCA, playing Clarinet, and played from 2015 till now. Since then, she received multiple diplomas in music performance and composition. She is also a member of NOVOwinds as a Clarinetist, Pianist, and occasionally does arrangements and co-produces their trailers. Outside of music composition and production, she is also a piano teacher.
